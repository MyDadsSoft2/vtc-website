About

Drive, log, and enjoy the miles.

A simple VTC for ETS2 drivers who want a relaxed place to track jobs and share progress.

Scott's Transport was started in 2024 as a small UK-based virtual trucking company for Euro Truck Simulator 2.

We keep things straightforward: complete deliveries, log them through MileVox, and build up your mileage at your own pace.

There are no mandatory convoys or awkward activity rules. Just drive, stay respectful, and be part of the community.
