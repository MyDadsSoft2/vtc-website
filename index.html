Clean miles. Tight outfit.

Simple, logged, singleplayer haulage without the convoy noise.

Scott's Transport runs singleplayer freight ops across Europe — every job logged, timed, and rated through MileVox JobTracker. No multiplayer chaos, no leaderboard farmers, no convoy carnage at junctions.

It's a proper haulier set-up in miniature. Disciplined dispatch, real schedules, themed runs, and a sub-contracted operator picking up overflow when the fleet's already on the road.

If you care more about the drive than the drama — pulling into a depot at 3am with a clean delivery on the dot — you're in the right cab.
